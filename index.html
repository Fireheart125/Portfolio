About me

I am a UCSD undergraduate studying Cognitive Science with a specialization in Human Computer Interaction (HCI). I was born and raised in LA County where I spent my days playing around with my older sister. That being said, I grew up being strongly influenced by my Asian heritage and history in addition to American culture. This blend of cultures is what influences my designs in some parts as I prefer clean and seemingly simplistic user interfaces.

Recently, I've been working on a couple of projects for school classes that have kept me occupied in addition to my part time job on campus. My goal with these projects is to create aesthetically pleasing sites that also aim to help user interaction at the same time.
